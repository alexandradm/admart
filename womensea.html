Collage, wooden and metal frames.

An ongoing series of collages depicting women's dreams and aspirations to remove themselves from patriarchal society. Collages are made while listening to feminist scholars and authors on podcasts or through audiobooks. Playing with the gender normative images sourced from 1940s-70s magazines.

A counterpart to my Man and Land collage series.
